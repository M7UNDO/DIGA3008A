Goal Alignment

My target audience

The target audience for my site includes three key groups. Firstly, potential employers, who are on the website to engage with my past projects and learn about me in order to consider me for professional positions. Second my peers, fellow developers and people within the industry who may be viewing my work for the purpose of potential collaborations or networking opportunities. Lastly users who might be visiting my website seeking some inspiration into my design process.

My content

  • My home page will serve as the landing page, welcoming users and providing them with a brief introduction of who I am and guiding them to different sections of my website.
  • My blog page will feature a collection of my weekly blogs.
  • My essay page will focus on more academic writing and themes.
  • My portfolio page will be the centrepiece of my website, showcasing my game development projects and artwork. Users can click on a link to play my games and interact directly with my work.
  • My design page provides a behind-the-scenes look at my web development process, allowing users to see the steps I took in planning and building up my website from day one.
  • My about page will give user insight into who I am, my skills and experience, offering a comprehensive overview into professional background and my expertise. It will also include where I am based and how they can get in touch with me.

Reference Material

Likes

  • The design of the website is sleek and futuristic. The black and white colour palette and the bold typography give the site a modern look and feel.
  • The use of bold typography on different headings helps the website achieve its strong visual hierarchy. This contributes to its easy navigation.
  • The design of the website is very clean and minimalist. It does a good job of avoiding clutter, allowing the content to stand out.
  • The way in which the designers' skillset has been displayed on the website effectively outlines their range of skills and projects and showcases the designers' versatility. I think this is a good idea and could attract potential employers.
  • The way the website makes use of creative typography, such as the fragmented text. This adds an artistic touch to the design.

Dislikes

  • The website's design relies heavily on text; there is a lack of imagery. For the design of my website, I intend to use more images and icons. In this case, however, it does not take away from the website's appeal and impact.
  • The website's design makes use of a limited colour palette. Although the minimalism is a positive for this website. Adding some spots of colour could potentially make the website more engaging.

Typography

I want the typography of my website to have a clean and modern feel. I think this is reflected by my font choices below, which are Montserrat, which has a sleek geometric design, which gives it a contemporary look, which would be ideal for my heading and the body of my text. The modern aesthetic of the font is created by its balanced letterforms and its versatility, which also maintains a professional look, while Khand has a contemporary touch because of its use of clean lines and subtle characters that enhance the modern feel without compromising the user's readability. I think using either of these fonts or a combination of both will ensure that my website feels aesthetically pleasing and easy to read.
